  • Pizza Margherita

    Preparation Time: 2 hours

    Ingredients:

    • 1 pizza dough ball
    • 2/3 cup of tomato sauce
    • 1 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese
    • 8-10 fresh basil leaves
    • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Recipe:

    1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually around 500°F or 260°C).
    2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle on a floured surface.
    3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
    4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
    6. Bake the pizza for about 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
    7. Remove the pizza from the oven and immediately place fresh basil leaves on top.
    8. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza, season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.
    9. Enjoy the authentic taste of Pizza Margherita!

  • Carbonara Pasta

    Preparation Time: 30 minutes

    Ingredients:

    • 8 ounces of spaghetti or fettuccine
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
    • 4-5 ounces of pancetta or guanciale
    • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    • Freshly ground black pepper

    Recipe:

    1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve a cup of pasta cooking water and then drain the pasta.
    2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper.
    3. In a large skillet, cook pancetta or guanciale until crispy. Remove from heat and add minced garlic.
    4. Add the drained pasta to the skillet and toss to combine with the pancetta and garlic.
    5. Remove the skillet from heat, then quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta. Toss vigorously to create a creamy sauce. If it's too thick, add some of the reserved pasta cooking water.
    6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Pecorino Romano cheese and black pepper.
    7. Indulge in the rich and savory Carbonara Pasta!

  • Macaroni and Cheese

    Preparation Time: 45 minutes

    Ingredients:

    • 8 ounces of elbow macaroni
    • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 3 cups milk
    • 1/4 cup butter
    • 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    • 2 1/2 tablespoons butter
    • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
    • 1 pinch paprika

    Recipe:

    1. Cook macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain.
    2. In a saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium heat. Stir in enough flour to make a roux. Add milk to roux slowly, stirring constantly. Stir in cheeses, and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and the sauce is a little thick. Put macaroni in a large casserole dish and pour sauce over macaroni. Stir well.
    3. Melt butter or margarine in a skillet over medium heat. Add breadcrumbs and brown. Spread over the macaroni and cheese to cover. Sprinkle with a little paprika.
    4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Serve.
    5. Delight in the creamy goodness of Macaroni and Cheese!

  • American Tacos

    Preparation Time: 40 minutes

    Ingredients:

    • 1 lb ground beef
    • 1 packet taco seasoning mix
    • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
    • 1 cup diced tomatoes
    • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
    • 1/4 cup diced onions
    • 1/4 cup sour cream
    • 8 taco shells

    Recipe:

    1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
    2. Add the taco seasoning mix and water according to the packet instructions. Simmer until the mixture thickens.
    3. Warm the taco shells in the oven according to the package instructions.
    4. Assemble your tacos by spooning the seasoned beef into each shell, then top with cheese, diced tomatoes, lettuce, onions, and a dollop of sour cream.
    5. Enjoy your homemade American Tacos!

  • Steak

    Preparation Time: 25 minutes

    Ingredients:

    • 2 ribeye or sirloin steaks
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped

    Recipe:

    1. Preheat your grill or grill pan to high heat.
    2. Brush both sides of the steaks with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
    3. Grill the steaks for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare (adjust the time for your desired doneness).
    4. During the last minute of cooking, sprinkle minced garlic and fresh rosemary over the steaks and cook briefly.
    5. Remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
    6. Savor the delicious flavors of your grilled steak!

  • Moussaka

    Preparation Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

    Ingredients:

    • 1 lb ground beef or lamb
    • 2 eggplants, sliced
    • 3 large potatoes, sliced
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 cups tomato sauce
    • 1/2 cup red wine
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
    • 1/4 cup butter
    • 4 cups milk
    • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

    Recipe:

    1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or lamb until browned. Add onions and garlic and sauté until they're soft and translucent.
    3. Stir in the tomato sauce and red wine. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg. Simmer for about 15 minutes.
    4. In a separate pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour to make a roux. Gradually whisk in the milk until the sauce thickens. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
    5. Arrange a layer of sliced eggplants in a greased baking dish. Top with half of the meat mixture, then add another layer of eggplants. Next, add a layer of sliced potatoes and the remaining meat mixture. Finish with another layer of eggplants.
    6. Pour the white sauce over the top and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and breadcrumbs.
    7. Bake for about 45-50 minutes or until the moussaka is golden brown and bubbly.
    8. Allow it to cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your homemade Moussaka!
