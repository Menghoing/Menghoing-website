Delicious Cambodian Cuisine

Experience the Flavors of Cambodia

Explore the rich and diverse flavors of Cambodia with a delightful selection of traditional Cambodian dishes.

From aromatic curries to fresh and flavorful ingredients, Cambodian cuisine offers a unique culinary journey that will tantalize your taste buds.

  • Num Banh Chok

    Preparation Time: 45 minutes

    Ingredients:

    • Rice noodles
    • Fish-based curry paste
    • Fresh vegetables (e.g., cucumber, banana flower, water spinach)
    • Bean sprouts
    • Herbs (e.g., mint, basil)
    • Pickled greens

    Recipe:

    1. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions.
    2. Prepare the fish-based curry paste or use a ready-made one.
    3. Blanch fresh vegetables and bean sprouts.
    4. Serve cooked noodles with fish-based curry paste, fresh vegetables, bean sprouts, herbs, and pickled greens.
    5. Enjoy your delicious Num Banh Chok!

  • Lok Lak Khmer

    Preparation Time: 30 minutes

    Ingredients:

    • Beef (sirloin or tenderloin), thinly sliced
    • Marinade (soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, garlic, and black pepper)
    • Fresh vegetables (e.g., lettuce, cucumber, tomato, and onion)
    • Lime or lemon wedges

    Recipe:

    1. Marinate the beef slices in a mixture of soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, minced garlic, and black pepper for about 15-30 minutes.
    2. Heat a pan or wok and stir-fry the marinated beef until it's cooked to your desired level of doneness.
    3. Serve the beef over a bed of fresh vegetables and garnish with lime or lemon wedges.
    4. Enjoy your savory Lok Lak Khmer!

  • Cambodian Porridge

    Preparation Time: 40 minutes

    Ingredients:

    • Rice
    • Chicken or fish (your choice)
    • Ginger
    • Garlic
    • Shallots
    • Black pepper
    • Green onions

    Recipe:

    1. Cook rice with water to make a thick porridge.
    2. Add your choice of chicken or fish, ginger, garlic, and shallots.
    3. Season with black pepper and simmer until the protein is cooked.
    4. Serve hot, garnished with green onions.
    5. Enjoy your comforting Cambodian Porridge!

  • Amok Trey

    Preparation Time: 50 minutes

    Ingredients:

    • Fish (snapper or catfish), thinly sliced
    • Coconut milk
    • Curry paste (lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, turmeric, garlic, and shallots)
    • Shrimp paste
    • Fish sauce
    • Palm sugar
    • Chili paste (optional)

    Recipe:

    1. Mix curry paste, shrimp paste, fish sauce, palm sugar, and coconut milk to create a fragrant paste.
    2. Coat fish slices with the paste and steam until the fish is cooked.
    3. Serve with steamed rice, garnished with chili paste (if desired).
    4. Enjoy your creamy and flavorful Amok Trey!

  • Kuy Trev

    Preparation Time: 35 minutes

    Ingredients:

    • Fish (snakehead or catfish)
    • Minced pork
    • Shrimp paste
    • Lemongrass
    • Kaffir lime leaves
    • Galangal
    • Turmeric
    • Garlic

    Recipe:

    1. Prepare a paste by blending shrimp paste, minced pork, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, galangal, turmeric, and garlic.
    2. Stir-fry the paste and add fish slices.
    3. Serve your Kuy Trev with rice.
    4. Enjoy the rich and aromatic flavors!

  • Beef Skew with Pickle

    Preparation Time: 40 minutes

    Ingredients:

    • Beef (sirloin or ribeye), thinly sliced
    • Pickle marinade (lime juice, garlic, fish sauce, and sugar)
    • Assorted vegetables for pickling (e.g., cucumber, carrot, and daikon radish)
    • Cilantro and mint leaves
    • Skewers

    Recipe:

    1. Marinate beef slices in a mixture of lime juice, minced garlic, fish sauce, and sugar for about 30 minutes.
    2. Prepare a separate pickling mixture by combining lime juice, sugar, and water. Add assorted vegetables and let them pickle for about 30 minutes.
    3. Thread marinated beef onto skewers and grill until cooked to your desired level of doneness.
    4. Serve with pickled vegetables, cilantro, and mint leaves.
    5. Enjoy the delightful Beef Skew with Pickle!
